# Sign of Ill Omen
*Source: Player's Handbook p. 111. Available in the SRD.*  

**Prerequisite**: 5th-level

You can cast [bestow curse](5E2014官方资源/spells/bestow-curse.md) once using a warlock spell slot. You can't do so again until you finish a long rest.
