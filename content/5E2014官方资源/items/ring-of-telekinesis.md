# Ring of Telekinesis
*Ring, major, very rare (requires attunement)*  


While wearing this ring, you can cast the [telekinesis](5E2014官方资源/spells/telekinesis.md) spell at will, but you can target only objects that aren't being worn or carried.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide p. 193. Available in the SRD.*
