# Darkvision
*2nd-level, Transmutation*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate
- **Duration:** 8 hour

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has [darkvision](5E2014官方资源/规则/senses.md#darkvision) out to a range of 60 feet.

**Classes**: [Artificer](5E2014官方资源/classes/artificer-tce.md), [Druid](5E2014官方资源/classes/druid.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](5E2014官方资源/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Monk (Way of Shadow)](5E2014官方资源/classes/monk-way-of-shadow.md), [Ranger](5E2014官方资源/classes/ranger.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](5E2014官方资源/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer](5E2014官方资源/classes/sorcerer.md), [Wizard](5E2014官方资源/classes/wizard.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook p. 230. Available in the SRD.*
